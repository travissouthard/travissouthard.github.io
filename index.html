About Me

I am a full stack software engineer looking to build applications and programs that improve people’s quality of life. I am particularly interested in working on projects that provide the user a practical service and a positive experience.

With these new programming skills, I want to build a career where I can build useful and beautiful applications and programs that improve people’s quality of life.
